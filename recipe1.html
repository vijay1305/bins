How to make potato fry?

Just follow the following steps...

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Cup Potatoes Cubed(about 2 large)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp oil or as needed
  • 1/4 tsp. mustard
  • 1/2 tsp cumin or jeera
  • 1 to 2 garlic cloves chopped or crushed
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal(optional)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 red chili dried broken(optional)
  • pinch asafoetida or hing
  • 1/8 tsp turmeric or haldi
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp GARAM MASALA or sambar powder
  • salt as needed

STEPS TO PREPARE:

  1. Wash potatoes and peel. Cube to small bite sizes about half inch cubes. Keep them in a bowl of water until used.Then help to remove the excess starch and prevents them from sticking to each other.
  2. Crush or chop garlic.Rinse curry leaves and set aside.
  3. Pour oil to a heavy bottom non stick or cast iron pan. When the oil turns hot,add cumin,mustard,red chili and dals.Fry dal till golden.
  4. Add curry leaves and garlic.When the garlic is lightly fried,add hing.
  5. Drain the water from potatoes and then add them to the pan.sprinkle turmeric. Mix and fry potatoes for about 3 to 4 minutes on a medium heat stirring often
  6. Lower the flame,cover and cook till the potatoes are done.Stir in between to prevent burning. If needed sprinkle some water in between.You can add also more oil for better roasting
  7. When the potatoes are roasted and cooked well.Check if they are cooked completely. You shouldn't feel hard from inside,Otherwise sprinkle some more water and fry
  8. Then sprinkle chili powder,salt,garam masala.Toss everything well and fry for a minute or two to blend well, Serve potato fry or alloo fry with rice and rasam
