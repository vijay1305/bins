"How to make potato fry?"

I like potato fry much because potatoes are loaded with starchy goodness and the fry oil is chock full of fats and oils, all of which are a caloric bomb that gives our bodies enough energy to do anything.

Just follow the following steps...

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 Cup cubed potatoes

  • 1 1/2 tsp oil

  • 1/4 tsp mustard
  • 1/2 tsp jeera
  • 1 to 2 garlic cloves chopped or crushed
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal(optional)
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 red chili dried broken
  • pinch asafoetida or hing
  • 1/8 tsp turmeric or haldi
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp GARAM MASALA or sambar powder
  • salt as needed

STEPS TO PREPARE:

  1. Wash potatoes and peel. Cube to small bite sizes about half inch cubes. Keep them in a bowl of water until used.Then help to remove the excess starch and prevents them from sticking to each other.😝😝
  2. Crush or chop garlic.Rinse curry leaves and set aside.😆😆
  3. Pour oil to a heavy bottom non stick or cast iron pan. When the oil turns hot,add cumin,mustard,red chili and dals.Fry dal till golden,add curry leaves.😤😤
  4. Add potatoes and turmeric.😁😁
  5. Fry about 3 to 4 minutes.😃😃
  6. Lower the flame,cover and cook till the potatoes are done.Stir in between to prevent burning. If needed sprinkle some water in between.You can add also more oil for better roasting.😎😎
  7. Then sprinkle chili powder,salt,garam masala.😋😋😋:
  8. Toss everything well and fry for a minute or two to blend well.😍😍😍
  9. Serve potato fry or alloo fry with rice and rasam.❤❤❤
